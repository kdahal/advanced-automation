BHAKTI RAS AMRITA SANGA

We welcome everyone

Bhakti Ras Amrita Sanga is a unique program that provides an opportunity for devotees to deepen their spiritual practice and become more connected to the Krishna Consciousness. Through this program, devotees can learn about the teachings of Krishna Consciousness, receive spiritual guidance, and participate in devotional service activities. The program also provides a platform for devotees to communize with each other and share their experiences in devotional service. By participating in Bhakti Ras Amrita Sanga, devotees can gain a deeper understanding of Krishna Consciousness and become more devoted to Sri Sri Radha Govinda’s service.

OUR MISSION

We are committed to providing a safe and welcoming environment for all devotees to learn and practice Krishna Consciousness. We strive to create an atmosphere of love, respect, and understanding for all who come to the temple.

We will provide a variety of activities that will help devotees deepen their understanding of Krishna Consciousness and its teachings, such as Introduction to Krishna Consciousness and it’s principles, Yoga Meditation Class, Training in Daily Sadhana, Open Discussion on Vedic Science Philosophy, Archa Vigrah courses, Discourses on Different Krishna Consciousness related Topics, Group Mantra Meditations Session, and Q&A Session with Great Krishna Consciousness Speakers and more. Our goal is to create an environment where devotees can come together in unity and harmony, while learning about the beauty of Krishan Consciousness.

We are dedicated to providing quality programs that will help devotees grow spiritually and emotionally. We will strive to provide a variety of activities that will help devotees gain knowledge about Krishna Consciousness, as well as develop their spiritual practice. We will also work hard to ensure that our programs are accessible to all members of the community, regardless of their background or financial situation.

We believe that by providing these services we can help spread the message of Krishan Consciousness throughout Denver and beyond. We hope that our efforts will bring more people into the fold of Krishna Consciousness, allowing them to experience its beauty and grace firsthand.
